Step 1.6: Make the next dilutions.

1. 100µl of first dilution to the next dilution (e.g., 10-2).
Transfer 100µl of first dilution to the next dilution (e.g., 10-2).
2. Vortex the second dilution and repeat the procedure with next dilutions.

Do not forget to close the lid, vortex and change the pipette tips after each addition!
